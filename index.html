Projects

Blights Out GitHub Live

Blights Out is a 2d top down game where you play an altered version of 'red light, green light'. The player controls a child walking down a long hallway at night to get to their parents' room with a monster following behind them. When the player hears the monster drawing near they have to turn around and look at the monster to make it retreat. The player wins if they make it to the parents' room without getting caught.

Picka Pet GitHub Live

Picka Pet is a fullstack MERN application that lets users adopt virtual pets to play with. Logged in in users are given the opportunity to pick from a diverse selection dogs and cats. Pet adopters have the option to play with their selected pet(s) through a variety of interactive options. Pet interactions lets users earn points which they can use to buy more pets. Picka Pet encourages users to be responsible before making the commitment to adopt a new pet

BoardGameFreak GitHub Live

BoardGameFreak, a BoardGameGeek clone, is an online forum for board gaming hobbyists. On BoardGameFreak, a user can sign-up/log-in, browse games in the database, post lists of their favorite games, and comment/like other users' lists

About

I now have a portfolio of work that I'm extremely proud of, a skillset that I improve upon everyday, and an excitement for what my future in software engineering holds.
